RECOMMENDATIONS

  • Allocate resources and marketing efforts towards the store channel, as it accounts for the highest percentage of profit (57%). This suggests that customers prefer the in-person shopping experience and are more likely to make purchases at physical locations.

  • Invest in expanding your online presence and enhancing your e-commerce capabilities. The online channel generates 21% of the profit, indicating that there is a significant opportunity for growth in the digital space. Consider optimizing your website, improving online customer service, and utilizing digital marketing strategies to attract and retain online customers.

  • Explore partnerships with resellers to expand your distribution network and reach a broader customer base. The reseller channel accounts for 13% of the profit, indicating that collaborating with resellers can help increase sales and revenue.
